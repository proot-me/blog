Welcome to our new blog

The purpose of this blog is to document the development of PRoot/CARE for posterity. The goal is to express any concerns or future enhancements in a more informal setting.

Keep in mind, this is in no way a replacement for documentation, which can be found on the website, and in the doc/ directory within the source code for PRoot.

Finally, real-time collaboration happens in the gitter channel, and support can also be found on both the mailing list, and the issue tracker.
